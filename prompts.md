# Prompt Engineering Documentation

## Project

**Business:** Natural Fitness Center

**Task:** AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses

---

# Prompt Version 1

## Prompt

Write website content for a gym.

### Observation

This prompt is too generic. It does not specify the business name, target audience, tone, or the required website sections, resulting in broad and less relevant output.

---

# Prompt Version 2

## Prompt

Write website content for a local gym named **Natural Fitness Center** located in Hyderabad.

Include:
- Hero Section
- About Us
- Services
- Why Choose Us
- Testimonials
- FAQs
- Contact Section

Use a professional and motivational tone.

### Improvement

This version introduces the business name, location, required website sections, and tone, producing more focused and relevant content.

# Prompt Version 3 (Final Prompt)

## Prompt

You are an expert website copywriter specializing in fitness businesses.

Create professional website copy for **Natural Fitness Center**, a local gym located in Hyderabad, India.

Business Details:
- Industry: Gym & Fitness
- Target Audience: Students, working professionals, beginners, and fitness enthusiasts.
- Goal: Increase memberships and encourage visitors to book a free trial.

Generate:
1. Hero Section
2. About Us
3. Why Choose Us
4. Services
5. Testimonials
6. FAQs
7. Final Call-to-Action

Requirements:
- Use a motivational, friendly, and trustworthy tone.
- Keep the content concise and website-ready.
- Highlight customer benefits instead of only features.
- Naturally include fitness-related keywords for SEO.

### Why This Prompt Is Better

- Clearly defines the AI's role.
- Provides complete business context.
- Specifies the target audience.
- Sets the desired tone.
- Requests a structured output.
- Includes quality constraints to improve the AI-generated content.
